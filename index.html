PINE LAKE ROBOTICS

Bio

Welcome to the Pine Lake Robotics Club! Our club is comprised of four sub-teams: software, build, media, and business. Each of these teams has an independent yet unique role to play in the development in group projects. Our mission is to foster a diverse and inclusive community where individuals can come together and collaborate on exciting projects. We strive to not only build a successful team, but also to create a supportive environment that will continue to inspire future members. At Pine Lake Robotics Club, we value teamwork, creativity, and innovation. Join us on our journey to push the boundaries of robotics and create a better tomorrow!

Our Robot

Introducing our cutting-edge FIRST Robotics Challenge (FRC) robot! Standing 18 inches tall, our marvel of engineering boasts a sturdy chassis, powerful drive system, and advanced sensors for precise navigation. With complex code and skilled programming, our robot can perform various tasks, from picking up game pieces to climbing obstacles. Created by our talented team of students, mentors, and sponsors, our robot is the result of hard work, creativity, and teamwork. Check out our competition videos for a glimpse of our robot in action and stay tuned for updates on our progress throughout the season!

Our Mentors

Our mentors at Pine Lake drive our FRC robot with expertise and unwavering support, fostering personal and technical growth. Grateful for their tireless efforts, we've learned valuable skills in mechanical, electrical, software, and strategy aspects, shaping us into capable engineers and leaders. Our robot is a testament to their commitment and passion, showcasing their mentorship. We are proud to have them as our guiding force in the FIRST Robotics Challenge, and their impact extends far beyond the competition season. Their mentorship has instilled in us a lifelong love for STEM.
