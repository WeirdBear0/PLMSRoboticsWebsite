Bio

Welcome to the Pine Lake Robotics Club! Our club is comprised of four sub-teams: software, build, media, and business. Each of these teams has an independent yet unique role to play in the development in group projects. Our mission is to foster a diverse and inclusive community where individuals can come together and collaborate on exciting projects. We strive to not only build a successful team, but also to create a supportive environment that will continue to inspire future members. At Pine Lake Robotics Club, we value teamwork, creativity, and innovation. Join us on our journey to push the boundaries of robotics and create a better tomorrow!

Our Robot

We are proud to present our general FIRST Robotics Challenge (FRC) robot. Our robot is a cutting-edge marvel of engineering and innovation, carefully crafted by our talented team of students, mentors, and sponsors. Standing at 18 inches tall, our robot features a sturdy chassis and powerful drive system, allowing it to navigate the competition field with ease. Equipped with an impressive array of sensors, including cameras, gyroscopes, and ultrasonic sensors, our robot has keen perception and precise navigation capabilities. With a high-performance onboard computer running complex code written by our skilled student programmers, our robot can perform a wide range of tasks, from picking up and manipulating game pieces to climbing obstacles and scoring points. Our robot is the result of countless hours of hard work, creativity, and teamwork, and we can't wait to see it in action at this year's FRC competition! Check out our robot in action in our competition videos and stay tuned for updates on our progress throughout the season.

Our Mentors

Our mentors at Pine Lake are the heart and soul behind our general FIRST Robotics Challenge (FRC) robot. As industry professionals with a wealth of experience, they provide us, the students, with invaluable guidance and expertise throughout the entire design, build, and programming process. Their unwavering support and dedication have been instrumental in helping us bring our robot to life. With their mentorship, we have learned the ins and outs of mechanical, electrical, software, and strategy aspects, gaining practical skills that will serve us well in our future careers. Their patient guidance and encouragement have fostered our personal and technical growth, teaching us problem-solving, critical thinking, and teamwork skills along the way. We are grateful for their tireless efforts and expertise, which have shaped us into capable engineers and leaders. Our robot is a testament to their commitment and passion, and we are proud to showcase it as a result of their unwavering mentorship.
