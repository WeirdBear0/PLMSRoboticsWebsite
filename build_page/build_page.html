Who We Are

We are the build team, a team comprised of several 3D modelers and designers who work together to ensure that our robots work correctly and look amazing.

What We Do

The build team starts at the whiteboard, brainstorming the pros and cons of all the different mechanisms they can add to the robot. The 3-D modelers of the team use Computer-Aided Design on a site called Onshape to make a realistic model of all of the potential features. Then they get to work, assembling, screwing, and putting together a competition-ready robot. Build team members learn critical components of engineering and design, which will be applicable all throughout their lives.
